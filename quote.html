Daily Devotional

POWER DEVOTIONAL

Job 36:11 If they obey and serve him, they shall spend their days in prosperity, and their years in pleasures.
Days are shorter than years. Your day is your youthful season whereas your years is your adult and aging times. That time is when pleasure is actually pleasurable.
Unfortunately, my generation is more concerned about their days not minding what becomes of their years.
There are seasons when you will eat for pleasure but there are times when you need to eat just to get the stomach filled. There are seasons where you can eat what you want but there are also seasons when you need to eat what is available. There are seasons when all you need is just survival. Hear me! "In your days of survival don't try to please anybody. Survive first, then we can settle later." Know this and know peace. If you don't understand these seasons, life can become difficult. But you see, the success and the otherwise of these seasons (days and years) are anchored on your service. "If they will serve Him"... if you don't have a service you rendering, you make it difficult for God to bless you.
Tonight, I ask the heavens on your behalf to grant unto you grace for delightful, delighting, joyful, diligent, dutiful, meaningful service in Jesus precious name.
I place a demand on heaven on your behalf tonight, may you spend your days in prosperity and your years in pleasure in Jesus precious name.
God bless you.

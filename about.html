WELCOME TO BEULAH CHRISTIAN ASSEMBLY INT'L

The mandate is what we refer to as the ministry. That was where everything began. It was delivered to our esteemed man of God, Pastor Love Emwinghare in his little room while still a student on campus in Federal Polytechnic, Auchi. He was on a 3 day adventure with God in prayer and fasting in that little room on 10th of October, 2010. He was in search for the spiritual welfare of the flocks under is care as a privileged Prayer Secreatry of Christ Ambassador Outreach (CASOR) when a voice spoke and said, "My assignment for you is not onlybfor these people but for the whole world. I am giving you three commission, at my Word, at my Name and at my Authority you will drive whatever trouble your people." For eight years we waited until the time was right.

One beautiful Sunday afternoon after church service in the month of November, 2017, I was on my way from church in a commuter bus, God said to me, "lift up your head and immediately I did, I saw an inscription in the sky, *"A NEW BEGINNING"*. I went to meet my father in the Lord and discussed the matter of the ministry with him and he counselled me as appropriate.

I didn't actually see the ministry as a church when God first spoke to me in 2010 but as an Outreach Activities, causing revivals in the already existing churches. That we were already doing until that fateful Sunday afternoon.

In preparation to begin the work, I asked the Lord what name it shall be called and He led me to Isaiah 62:4 " Thou shalt no more be termed Forsaken; neither shall thy land any more be termed Desolate: but thou shalt be called Hephzibah, and thy land Beulah: for the LORD delighteth in thee, and thy land shall be married."
This is how the name BEULAH CHRISTIAN ASSEMBLY INT'L was birthed.
